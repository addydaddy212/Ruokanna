QA Citrus Chicken Bowl

A bright, high-protein chicken rice bowl with spinach and lemon yogurt.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1/2 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken and sear it in a hot pan until cooked through.
  2. Warm the rice, toss the spinach, and stir lemon juice into the yogurt.
  3. Slice the chicken and build the bowls with rice, spinach, yogurt, and lemon.
